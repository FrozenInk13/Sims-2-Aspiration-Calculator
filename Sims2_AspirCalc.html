Sims 2 Aspiration Calculator

Welcome to my Sims 2 Aspiration Calculator! If you want to calculate a Sim's Aspiration, Secondary Aspiration, and Jealousy Level for when you're playing the Sims 2, then feel free to use this site to help determine that! The calculation takes the Sim's parent's Aspirations, (both primary and secondary) the Sims's personality, the Sim's interests, and the Sim's zodiac sign into consideration. While the Primary and Secondary Aspirations will remain constant no matter how many times you calculate it, the Jealousy Level will be random while taking into account the Primary and Secondary Aspiration your Sim would get. Feel free to ignore the Jealousy Levels if you wish to not play with them.

To use this calculator, just input your Sim's data into every spot below. The calculator might give an inaccurate result if you don't ensure every category visible to you is filled out properly, so if you care about that then make sure you went through every option before hitting the "calculate" button at the bottom. Your results will show up below the "calculate" and "reset" buttons, so if you don't see them after pressing it you might need to scroll a bit first. Pressing the "reset" button will restore everything to how it was when you first entered the page. I hope you have fun using this tool!
